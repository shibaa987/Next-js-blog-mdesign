Mehndi is an important part of many special occasions in India, Pakistan and other countries in South Asia. It is not only used for weddings, but also for festivals like Diwali and Eid. A beautiful Mehndi design can make your occasion even more special.

There are many different types of Mehndi designs to choose from. If you are looking for something traditional, then a simple paisley or floral design may be perfect for you. If you want something a little more modern, then there are lots of intricate designs available.
